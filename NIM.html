The game starts with a stack of nimbs. This is a nimb: . On each turn you'll take one to three nimbs off the stack, then your opponent will do the same. The player who takes the last nimb off the stack is the winner.


You will need to chose how many nimbs you want to play with, and decide if you want to play with a friend or against the computer.
